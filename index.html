DMS and North Atlantic Right Whales:

The food chain and chemicals to understand whale feeding and migration patterns

Click on the images to learn more about the relationship between DMS and North Atlantic Right Whales!

This research is critical for protecting endangered species like the North Atlantic Right Whale, with only 372 individuals remaining as of October 2024. If whales and DMS levels can be linked, ships could use sensor systems to detect high DMS areas, avoiding vessel strikes and saving these incredible marine giants!
